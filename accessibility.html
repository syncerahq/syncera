Accessibility Statement

Digital access matters here.

Syncera Intelligence is committed to making its website and research participation experience accessible to as many users as possible.

We are actively working to improve accessibility and usability, with the goal of aligning with WCAG 2.1 Level AA where reasonably possible.

Last updated: April 2026

What we are doing
  • Using semantic page structure and readable contrast.
  • Supporting keyboard navigation and visible focus states.
  • Using labeled form controls and progress indicators.
  • Providing privacy and accessibility information in public-facing pages.
Need help?

If you experience difficulty accessing content, completing the survey, or navigating the site, contact us at:

syncerahq@gmail.com

We will make reasonable efforts to address your concern and provide the information you need in an accessible format.
