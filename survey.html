Content trust perception study

This study measures whether official U.S. film ratings match what real audiences say they actually experience — including intensity, tone, and mismatch between label and reality.

You may also share the most recent film you watched so Syncera can begin building a title-level live pulse over time.

What to expect

Approximately 6–8 minutes. No right or wrong answers. Please respond based on your genuine experience.

What makes this different

This study is about accuracy, not popularity. It captures whether a film matched expectations — not whether you liked it.

Informed Consent — Participation is voluntary and anonymous. No personally identifying information is collected. Responses may be used in aggregated, anonymized form for research, publications, title-level pulse summaries, and policy or business discussion by Syncera Intelligence. By proceeding, you confirm you are 13 years of age or older.
