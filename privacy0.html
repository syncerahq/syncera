Privacy Policy

Your trust matters here.

Syncera Intelligence collects audience perception data to help understand where media labels, ratings, and recommendations match — or miss — the viewer experience.

This page explains what we collect, what we do not collect, and how survey responses are used.

Effective date: April 2026

What we collect

When you submit a survey, we may collect the answers you provide, including movie-viewing habits, opinions about ratings, optional title references, optional demographic information, optional written feedback, and basic technical submission data needed to process the form.

What we do not intentionally collect

We do not ask for your name, home address, phone number, Social Security number, or payment information. Please do not include personal identifying information in open-response fields.

How responses are used

Responses may be used in aggregated and anonymized form for research, reporting, internal analysis, future study design, title-level pulse summaries, and discussions related to media trust, content labeling, and audience perception.

Third-party processing

Survey submissions are currently processed using a third-party form service so responses can be delivered to Syncera Intelligence for review and analysis.

Children’s privacy

This survey is intended only for people who are 13 years of age or older. If you are under 13, please do not submit the survey.

Contact

Questions can be sent to syncerahq@gmail.com.
