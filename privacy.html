Privacy Policy

Your trust matters here.

Syncera Intelligence is committed to protecting the privacy of people who visit this site and participate in our research. This page explains what we collect, what we do not collect, and how survey responses are used.

Effective date: April 2026

1. What this site is for

Syncera Intelligence conducts independent audience perception research. Our current public survey collects responses about film rating perception and related media trust topics.

2. What we collect

When you submit a survey, we may collect the responses you provide, such as:

  • Film viewing habits
  • Opinions about rating systems
  • Optional demographic information
  • Optional written responses
  • Basic technical submission data needed to process the form

3. What we do not intentionally collect

  • Your name
  • Your home address
  • Your phone number
  • Your Social Security number
  • Payment information

Please do not include personal identifying information in any open-response field.

4. How your responses are used

Survey responses may be used in aggregated and anonymized form for research, reporting, publications, internal analysis, future study design, and policy or business discussions related to media trust, content classification, and audience perception.

We look for patterns across responses. We do not publish your individual identity because we are not asking for it.

5. How submissions are processed

Survey submissions are currently processed using a third-party form service. That means your responses may pass through that service so they can be delivered to Syncera Intelligence for review and analysis.

While we aim to use privacy-conscious tools, third-party services may have their own privacy and data-handling practices.

6. Cookies and analytics

This site may use basic hosting or platform-level technical logs to operate and secure the site. At this stage, we are not using aggressive ad tracking or behavioral advertising tools on respondents.

7. Children’s privacy

This survey is intended only for people who are 13 years of age or older. If you are under 13, please do not submit the survey.

8. Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect submitted information, but no website or online transmission method can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. By participating, you understand that online submission always carries some level of risk.

9. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as Syncera Intelligence grows, adds tools, or changes research practices. The most current version will always appear on this page.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or about a Syncera Intelligence study, you can contact:
