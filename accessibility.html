Accessibility Statement

Digital access matters here.

Syncera Intelligence is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, including individuals with disabilities.

We are actively working to improve the accessibility and usability of our website and aim to align with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards.

Last updated: April 2026

What we are doing

  • Using clear page structure and readable text contrast.
  • Supporting keyboard navigation and visible focus states.
  • Improving form usability for screen reader and keyboard users.
  • Providing accessibility and privacy information in public-facing pages.

Need help or found an issue?

If you experience any difficulty accessing content, completing the survey, or navigating the site, please contact us at:

syncerahq@gmail.com

We will make reasonable efforts to address your concerns and provide the information you need in an accessible format.
