Content Trust Research · Independent Study · 2026

The signal between what media claims to be and what you actually experience.
We're measuring it.

Seven in ten Americans no longer trust what they consume in media. Syncera Intelligence is collecting the first demographic dataset on where official content classifications diverge from real audience experience. Your voice is the data.

Takes approximately 6–8 minutes. No right or wrong answers. Please respond based on your genuine experience.

Informed Consent — Participation is voluntary and completely anonymous. No personally identifying information is collected. Responses will be used in aggregated, anonymized form for research, publications, and policy advocacy by Syncera Intelligence. By proceeding you confirm you are 13 years of age or older.
